What should an asset manager monitor?

Fee changes · product launches · new wrappers · investment-universe changes · ESG positioning · published performance methodology · partnerships · public AUM.

Methodology & Limitations

  • Volatility: daily standard deviation × √252.
  • Maximum drawdown: worst decline from a previous peak.
  • Tracking error: annualised standard deviation of ETF minus benchmark daily returns.
  • Tracking difference: ETF annualised return minus benchmark annualised return.
  • Yahoo Finance is not an institutional data source and may be delayed or unavailable.
  • TER, AUM, replication, SFDR and competitor information require manual verification.
  • No investment recommendation is provided.
